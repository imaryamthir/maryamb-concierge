Our Journey

From Dream to Reality

2020 · The Beginning

A Vision Takes Shape

Maryam, a fashion enthusiast from Lahore, saw a gap — Pakistani women deserved luxury fashion that celebrated their heritage without compromising on modern sophistication. With just 10 designs and a dream, Maryam B was born.

2021 · First Collection

Noor — The Debut

The launch of the "Noor" collection — 30 handcrafted pieces blending traditional embroidery with contemporary silhouettes. It sold out within 72 hours, and the world took notice.

2022–23 · Growth

Expanding the Universe

Bridal consultations, international shipping to the UAE & UK, accessories lines, and a growing community of 50,000+ women who trust Maryam B for their most important moments.

2025 · Today & Beyond

AI-Powered Luxury Fashion

Launching AI-powered styling, exclusive flagship experiences, and a sustainability pledge. The mission remains unchanged — to make every Pakistani woman feel extraordinary.

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery within Pakistan takes 3–5 working days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for major cities like Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad. International shipping to UAE and UK takes 7–10 days.
What is the return policy? +
We offer a 14-day hassle-free exchange and return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags attached. Bridal and sale items are final sale. Contact us via WhatsApp to initiate a return.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery? +
Yes! We offer COD across all major cities in Pakistan. A small handling fee of PKR 100 may apply. For orders above PKR 10,000, advance payment is required.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes, we ship to UAE, UK, USA, Canada, and Saudi Arabia. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout. Delivery takes 7–14 business days depending on location.
How do I find my size? +
Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements in both inches and centimeters. We recommend measuring your bust, waist, and hips for the best fit. Our WhatsApp team can also help you choose the right size.
Can I track my order? +
Absolutely! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can also use our Order Tracking page on the website to check real-time updates.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), bank transfers, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and Cash on Delivery. All online payments are secured with SSL encryption.
Do you offer bridal consultations? +
Yes! We offer personalized bridal consultations via WhatsApp and video call. Our bridal specialist will guide you through fabric selection, customization options, and complete look coordination. Book via WhatsApp: +92 318 923 6341.
